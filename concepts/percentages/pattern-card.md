# Pattern Card: Percentages

## Use when:
- "X% of Y" → X% = X/100, multiply by Y
- "X is what % of Y?" → (X/Y) × 100
- Successive increases/decreases → multiply by (1 ± X/100)
- Profit/loss → (SP-CP)/CP × 100

## Core question:
"What is the base (100%) and what is the part?"

## Common mistakes:
- +25% then -25% ≠ 0 (it's -6.25%)
- Using wrong base: (new-old)/old, not /new
- Successive discounts: 20% + 15% ≠ 35%, use 1-0.8×0.85 = 0.32

## Interview hints:
- 25% = 1/4, 50% = 1/2, 75% = 3/4
- Profit on CP, not SP, unless stated
- Watch for equal increase/decrease (always net decrease)
