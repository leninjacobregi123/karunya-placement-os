# Answer Key

## Quiz Answers
### Q1
**Answer:** `30%`
Profit = 65 - 50 = ₹15. Profit% = (15/50) × 100 = 30%

### Q2
**Answer:** `₹1000`
SP = CP × 0.80, so CP = 800/0.80 = ₹1000

### Q3
**Answer:** `₹400`
Discount = 20% of 500 = ₹100. SP = 500 - 100 = ₹400

### Q4
**Answer:** `4% loss`
Equal profit/loss% always gives loss of (20/10)² = 4%. Total cost > total SP.

### Q5
**Answer:** `11.11%`
Gain% = (1000-900)/900 × 100 = 100/900 × 100 ≈ 11.11%
