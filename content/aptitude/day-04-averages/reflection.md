# Reflection Questions: Averages

1. If the average of 5 numbers is 100 and one number is 40, what is the average of the remaining 4 numbers? Can you verify your answer makes sense?

2. A new student joins a class of 20 students with average height 160 cm. The new class average becomes 162 cm. How tall is the new student?

3. Why can't you average two averages directly? For example, if class A has 10 students averaging 70 and class B has 20 students averaging 80, is the overall average 75?

4. When you add a number to a set, how does it change the average? Does it always increase?

5. A car travels 100 km at 50 km/h and returns at 100 km/h. Is the average speed 75 km/h? Calculate it properly.
