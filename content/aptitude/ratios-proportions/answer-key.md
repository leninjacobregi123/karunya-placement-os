# Answer Key

## Quiz Answers
### Q1
**Answer:** `576`
Girls = 5 parts = 240, so 1 part = 48. Boys = 7 × 48 = 336. Total = 336 + 240 = 576. Or: total parts = 12, total = 12 × 48 = 576.

### Q2
**Answer:** `6:8:9`
Common term is b. Make b the same: multiply 3:4 by 2 to get 6:8. Keep 8:9. So a:b:c = 6:8:9.

### Q3
**Answer:** `300g`
Sugar:Flour = 2:5. If flour (5 parts) = 750g, then 1 part = 150g. Sugar (2 parts) = 2 × 150 = 300g.

### Q4
**Answer:** `12`
Mean proportion = sqrt(4 × 36) = sqrt(144) = 12. Check: 4:12 = 12:36, both simplify to 1:3 = 1:3.

### Q5
**Answer:** `Rs 700`
Total parts = 7 + 8 = 15. Each part = 1500/15 = Rs 100. A's share = 7 × 100 = Rs 700.
