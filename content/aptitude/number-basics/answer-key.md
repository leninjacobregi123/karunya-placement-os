# Answer Key

## Quiz Answers
### Q1
**Answer:** `24`
24 ÷ 4 = 6 and 24 ÷ 6 = 4. 24 is the smallest number divisible by both.

### Q2
**Answer:** `12`
36 = 2² × 3², 48 = 2⁴ × 3. Common factors: 2² × 3 = 12

### Q3
**Answer:** `36`
12 = 2² × 3, 18 = 2 × 3². LCM = 2² × 3² = 36

### Q4

### Q5
**Answer:** `Sum of digits is divisible by 9`
A number is divisible by 9 if sum of its digits is divisible by 9. E.g., 126 → 1+2+6=9
