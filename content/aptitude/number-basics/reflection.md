# Sequence 01 Reflection Questions

## Think about your learning

1. What is the key difference between HCF and LCM, and why does HCF always use the lowest prime powers while LCM uses the highest?

2. Can a number be divisible by 6 but not by 3? Explain your reasoning using the definition of divisibility by 6.

3. When testing if 97 is prime, why do you only need to check divisors up to 9? What would happen if you stopped at 8?

4. You found that 1,234 is divisible by 2 but not by 9. Does this tell you anything about whether it is divisible by 4? Justify your answer.

5. In your own words, describe the fastest method you know to spot whether any 3-digit number is divisible by 11. How would you explain it to someone else?
